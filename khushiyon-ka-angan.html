Khushiyon Ka Angan - A CSR Initiative Grihum Housing Finance

"Khushiyon Ka Angan" stands as a compassionate endeavor with the purpose of supporting underprivileged young girls by aiding their education fees for the academic year.

We hold a strong belief in the pivotal roles these girls are destined to assume in shaping the future of our society. Our primary objective is to extend the essential assistance required to ensure they receive a well-deserved education, thereby nurturing an environment to unleash the inherent potential that can radiate, making a meaningful and affirmative impact on society.

Eligibility Criteria-

  1. Only Girl beneficiaries
  2. Age 5-21 years
  3. Citizen of India
  4. Parent’s Profession falls under Affordable Construction Ecosystem that includes Mason, Mistry, Construction Contractor, Construction Worker, Painter, Plumber, Electrician, Building Material Supplier, and other related professions.
  5. Passing Certificate of last academic year.
  6. Monthly Family Income maximum upto Rs. 50,000.
